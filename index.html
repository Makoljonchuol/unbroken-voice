Stories of Resilience from Refugee Children

About the Event

Unbroken Voices is an initiative to share the real stories, strength, and creativity of refugee children in Kenya.

Our project aims to shine a light on their challenges and achievements through digital storytelling, and inspires the broader community to support and celebrate their resilience.

This event is ideal for students, educators, and anyone interested in supporting youth empowerment and hearing powerful stories of hope.
